About Us

The Drone Gymnasium is a dedicated indoor facility designed for drone pilots of all skill levels. Whether you are a beginner learning to hover or an experienced racer pushing top speeds, our space has everything you need to fly safely and improve your skills.

Facilities

Open Flying Arena

A large open area for free flight, practice maneuvers, and freestyle flying.

Racing Track

A purpose-built racing course with gates, flags, and timing systems.

Training Zone

A beginner-friendly zone with obstacles designed for skill development.
