About Us

Welcome to the Drone Gymnasium! We provide a safe, controlled indoor environment where pilots of all skill levels can practice flying, improve their technique, and compete with others.

Whether you're a first-time flyer or an experienced racer, our facility has something for everyone.

Our Facilities

Training Arena

A spacious 5,000 sq ft open flight zone perfect for beginners learning the basics of drone control.

Racing Circuit

A dedicated FPV racing track with adjustable gates, obstacles, and a live timing system for competitive pilots.

Repair Workshop

A fully equipped workshop with tools and spare parts so you can fix your drone and get back in the air quickly.
